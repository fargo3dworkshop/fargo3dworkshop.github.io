1st FARGO3D Workshop

Hands-on applications

16-19 January 2024, Santiago de Chile

About

This workshop is dedicated to students and young researchers interested in the topic of hydrodynamical simulations in the context of protoplanetary disks. One of the main objectives of this workshop is to build and strengthen a community around the FARGO3D code. Further, the workshop aims to deepen the understanding of hydrodynamical processes in numerics, by combining sets of theoretical lectures with sessions of hands-on application, with topics spanning from theoretical hydrodynamics to the comparison with state-of-the-art observations.

A limited number of participants will be allowed, and we will prioritize students and early-career researchers.

Limited financial support may be allocated after the registration closes and will be available upon request. Unfortunately, we cannot offer financial support for travel expenses.

All participants must follow the code of conduct.

Program

The workshop will consist of lectures during the morning session (9-13) and hands-on practice during the afternoon (14-17). The topics will include numerical hydrodynamics, protoplanetary disk dynamics, planet-disk interactions, radiative transfer calculations and the connection with observations.
